PURCHASE OF LAND

In the COLONY, you can purchase land as NFT. The purchased land can be used as your digital land.

BUYING REAL ESTATE

You can purchase real estate as NFT in the Colony. In addition, you can open tenant stores to sell NFT items you have to your customers and advertise your company. You can also rent out the real estate you purchased and make money from it. In short, you can expand your real estate portfolio just like in the real world.

BUYING AVATAR ITEM

You can purchase fashion items and other items as NFTs, just like in the real world. The items you purchase will be valued and become one of your collections. Of course, you can also sell or trade them.

EARN MONEY WITH YOUR WORK

The tenants you open can sell the items you have as NFTs. You can receive the profits as CVN tokens, and you can convert it into real world money.

EARN MONEY BY PLAYING GAMES

There is a gaming space where you can play in the casino and earn currency in the colony. All players who want to enjoy the Metaverse easily can play CONNECT VISION for free. Players will also be able to play, earn and get CVN tokens and In-Game NFT items.

MARKETPLACE

Real-world companies can use the colony as a new sales promotion vehicle. This allows them to reach a previously untargeted customer base and expand the range of customers they can target.

ESTABLISHMENT OF THE TEAM

The CONNECT VISION begins its journey into the metaverse!
It does so by establishing a professional team of marketing experts, community managers, developers and tokenomics experts that will formulate the core team and our goals!
All of us have been involved in the crypto scene for a long time, and we hope to create a long term project that will help everyone to access the metaverse via a simple app!

MARKET RESEARCH INTO WEB3

After researching the web3 space, the CONNECT VISION founders came to a conclusion that the metaverse remains mostly inaccessible to many of users, especially those from developing countries.
Therefore, after conducting our research, we decided to pursue an app through which users could access an entire metaverse on their phone and engage in different activities, such as cryptocurrency transactions, gambling, staking and more- all at the push of a button.

DEVELOPMENT LAUNCH OF THE CONNECTVISION APP

The official start to the development of the metaverse that can be hosted within a simple smartphone app begins!
It is estimated by our development team to take up to Q3-Q4 for the beta release to be accessible, if the goals of fundraising the necessary resources are met. We hope to provide our community into insight into the dev process soon!

CREATION OF A MARKET ENTRY STRATEGY

We plan on consulting with web3 market experts on how to reach as many people as possible during our debut within this scene, as well as how to enter the metaverse sector as smoothly as possible.
The core team consists of veterans of the industry, however we would like to have external assurance as well to create a ripple effect that will get us noticed!

CREATION OF A VIABLE BUSINESS MODEL AND TOKENOMICS

We plan on creating a sustainable business that will be able to hold an entire ecosystem, ensuring the long term profitability of the entire enterprise for everyone involved: the team, investors, partners and our community.
