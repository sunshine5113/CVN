PURCHASE OF LAND

In the COLONY, you can purchase land as NFT. The purchased land can be used as your digital land.

BUYING REAL ESTATE

You can purchase real estate as NFT in the Colony. In addition, you can open tenant stores to sell NFT items you have to your customers and advertise your company. You can also rent out the real estate you purchased and make money from it. In short, you can expand your real estate portfolio just like in the real world.

BUYING AVATAR ITEM

You can purchase fashion items and other items as NFTs, just like in the real world. The items you purchase will be valued and become one of your collections. Of course, you can also sell or trade them.

EARN MONEY WITH YOUR WORK

The tenants you open can sell the items you have as NFTs. You can receive the profits as CVN tokens, and you can convert it into real world money.

EARN MONEY BY PLAYING GAMES

There is a gaming space where you can play in the casino and earn currency in the colony. All players who want to enjoy the Metaverse easily can play CONNECT VISION for free. Players will also be able to play, earn and get CVN tokens and In-Game NFT items.

MARKETPLACE

Real-world companies can use the colony as a new sales promotion vehicle. This allows them to reach a previously untargeted customer base and expand the range of customers they can target.
